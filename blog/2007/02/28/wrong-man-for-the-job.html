Wrong Man for the Job

Feb 28th, 2007

Amir Peretz continues to remind everyone why he is totally the wrong man for the job of defense minister of the State of Israel. The latest example of Peretz’s total ineptness as top military defender of the Jewish state came on Tuesday, when he said the IDF just may have to go back into Gaza soon because: > “There is no reason to allow the recent escalation [in rocket fire from Gaza] turn into a real threat to the State of Israel.” I beg your pardon? The almost daily firing of missiles on your citizens by those who propose to be your neighbors is not a threat to the sovereignty and present and future security of the state? What other nation on earth would allow its neighbors to daily lob missiles onto its civilian population centers while pretending that such aggression does not pose any “real threat” to its sovereignty and security? Don’t even get me started on the unbelievable photos of Peretz inspecting military exercises last week with the lens caps still on his binoculars! The blind defender of Israel!
