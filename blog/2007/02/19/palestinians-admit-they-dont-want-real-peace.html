Palestinians Admit They Don't Want Real Peace

Feb 19th, 2007

It always astonishes me how the international community simply refuses to take the Arabs at their word. Talk about racism! For example, the Bush administration continues to insist that the “Palestinian” general public wants peace with Israel based on the “two-state vision.” The “Palestinians” themselves are doing their best to refute that claim, but no one is listening! According to the results of a new “Palestinian”-conducted poll, 70% support peace with Israel, but only if that peace makes Israel disappear. You see, the “peace” they support - all 70% of them (the overwhelming majority of the general public) - is one based on a single state where Arabs (including the millions of so-called “refugees”) vastly outnumber Jews. Seventy-five percent of “Palestinians” said that Israel, the Jewish state, simply has no right to exist. Coupled with US Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice’s earlier revelation that the new “Palestinian” unity government is not going to meet even that most basic peace obligation of recognizing Israel, this should be enough to end the present “peace” process. And it should put to rest the stupid claims that Hamas, which was overwhelmingly voted into office, does not represent the true desires of the Palestinian Arabs when it comes to relations with Israel. As it turns out, Hamas is totally in tune with the “Palestinian” street.
