Rice Backs Suppression of Relgious Freedom in Jerusalem

Feb 20th, 2007

One Jerusalem has a petition going to protest US Secretary of State Condoleezza Rice’s apparent siding with the Muslims over the ridiculous controversy surrounding what amounts to fairly minor renovation work by Israel at the Temple Mount. According to the news wires: > Rice “says (Muslim) concerns about Israeli renovations near a revered Jerusalem mosque are justified.” One Jerusalem decries the fact that Rice is certainly aware that the work being done by the Israelis is coming nowhere near the foundations of the mosques atop the Temple Mount, and that the matter is being used as an excuse to fan the flames of anti-Israel violence. Likewise, they point out that Rice has never made a similar fuss over the Muslims’ enormous unauthorized excavations directly underneath the Temple Mount that has led to the destruction of centuries of Jewish history. I would add to that analysis that Rice, and therefore the United States government, is actually aiding in Islam’s suppression of religious freedom in Jerusalem. You see, the gate that the Israelis are trying to make accessible again is the only entrance to the Temple Mount that Jews and Christians are allowed to use. If that gate is made permanently unusable, then the Temple Mount - holiest site on earth to the Jews and many Christians - will become off-limits to anyone who is not a Muslim. Kind of like Mecca, but inside a democratic and free state.
