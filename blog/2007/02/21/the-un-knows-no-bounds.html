The UN Knows No Bounds

Feb 21st, 2007

At least not when it comes to demonizing Israel. I know I should not be surprised, but sometimes the audacity of the UN still causes me to lose some coffee through my nose. It happened again yesterday when I read that the UN anti-racism panel had met in a special session to question Israel over whether or not it protects the holy sites of religions other than Judaism. This is just totally unbelievable. There are mosques, churches and New Age centers scattered throughout this country, not to mention the central shrine of the Bahai faith in Haifa. And those are just the modern places of worship. What does the UN think all those millions of Christian pilgrims do when they come here every year? They visit Christian holy sites that are not only protected for that purpose, but are usually fixed up real pretty by the Israelis for their non-Jewish guests. The hypocrisy of the UN simply knows no bounds. Where was this stupid panel when the Muslims were destroying centuries worth of Jewish religious artifacts underneath the Temple Mount a few years ago? Where was this panel when Palestinian Arab mobs were destroying an ancient synagogue in Jericho or the burial site of one of Israel’s most revered patriarchs in Shechem? Where were they when tens of synagogues were burned to the ground in Gaza in the summer of 2005? And where are these UN hypocrites every time Saudi Arabia forbids a Christian from carrying a Bible into the country, or tortures its own people for becoming believers in Jesus? The examples could go on and on, but I think we all get the point. More outrage over this issue from Atlas Shrugs: UN Pimps for Islamazis in Attack on Jerusalem
