God Stopped Tel Aviv Bomber

Feb 21st, 2007

West Bank Mama has a take on yesterday’s thwarted Tel Aviv suicide bombing that is, with typical disregard for the divine, being ignored by the rest of the media. One of the most frustrating aspects of living in Israel is watching so many of the Israelis (the vast majority, I would say) totally disregard the hand of the Almighty in the defense of their nation. Conventional wisdom in Israel, especially among the ruling elite, is that Israel is in and of itself a mighty military force with no true rival in the region. So few stop to consider how in the world such a tiny nation with so few people and natural resources became so mighty as to be able to defeat the combined forces of much larger nations. Or how about the Israelis’ uncanny ability to prevent so many of the terrorist attacks planned against them? Why are the well-funded, heavily-armed and internationally-backed “Palestinian” terrorists not able to carry out attacks on Israel with nearly the same frequency or destructive power as the globally-shunned militants in Iraq? It can’t be that the IDF and Shin Bet are *that* much better than the US military. Someone Else must be playing a **big** role in Israel’s defense; it just doesn’t make sense otherwise. And it is a shame that more Israelis don’t see this. But they will. A day is coming that the strength of their own arms will fail (we started to see this already with the debacle in Lebanon last summer), and the nation will see and understand Who it is that defends them.
