Abdullah Can Blame Israel's 'Fortress Mentality' on His Own People

Mar 5th, 2007

Always the smooth talker, Jordan’s King Abdullah II on Friday said that Israel needs to choose between maintaining a “fortress mentality” and living in peaceful coexistence with its Arab neighbors. What Abdullah forgot to mention is that Israel today has a fortress mentality because its Arab neighbors have never demonstrated anything but a desire to annihilate the Jewish state. Kind of hard to not develop a fortress mentality when your neighbors are constantly trying to kill you. This is nothing but another Arab attempt to further turn the diplomatic tables on Israel and demonize its efforts to defend its citizens. More on this from Meryl Yourish, The American-Israeli Patriot, and israel matzav.
