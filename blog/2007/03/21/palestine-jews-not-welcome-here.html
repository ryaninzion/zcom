Palestine: Jews Not Welcome Here

Mar 21st, 2007

The takeover of an empty home in Hebron by new Jewish landlords and their tenants has presented yet another opportunity to unmask the wretchedness and racism of Palestinian Arab society toward those it claims to seek peace with. I am not going to get into the whole issue of who actually holds ownership of the house in question - the new Jewish occupants or the two different Arabs who claim the property as their own. Others have already addressed the matter rather effectively. What I want to do is use this story - which naturally, despite being such a relatively insignificant matter, is making headlines around the world because it involves “Jewish settlers” - to highlight the fact that the Palestinian Authority-controlled areas are one of the very few places on earth where selling property to Jews is a crime punishable by death. And it’s not just an informal threat of physical harm by purportedly uncontrollable rogue militants. The Palestinian Authority has actually declared the sale of property to Jews a crime according to the law of the land. Arafat once clarified the issue by telling reporters that the offending law is: > a Jordanian law that we inherited, which applies to both the West Bank and Gaza, and sets the death penalty for those who sell land to Israelis. … We are talking about a few traitors, and we shall implement against them what is written in the law books. It is actually on the books! The PA-appointed mufti of Jerusalem later made it a religious crime as well. And these are Israel’s peace partners! Who cares if the “West Bank” and Gaza are, in the minds of some, destined to be a separate and independent Arab-majority state. Are Jews to be forbidden to live and buy property there based on their ethnicity? Because if so, that raises some very troubling questions about the role of America and Europe in helping to birth an openly racist state.
