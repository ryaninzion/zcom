Arabs Persecuted for Loving Israel

Oct 2nd, 2007

As a further follow-up to my earlier post regarding a lovely Arab woman from northern Israel who loves Israel and the God of Israel with all her heart, I want to provide an example of how such servants of the Lord are being persecuted by their own people. This same woman who just days before embraced and was embraced by an Israeli Jewish woman from the rocket-battered town of Sderot was on Monday the target of Israeli-Arab Muslim aggression in Tel Aviv. As the International Christian Zionist Center Feast of Tabernacles group gathered outside Israeli army headquarters in Tel Aviv, we all noticed the conspicuous presence of an unhappy looking Arab woman in full Muslim attire. She seemed to be zoning in on the Christian Arab woman with us, who promptly notified the hovering Israeli security agents (imagine that - an Arab requesting that Israeli security come investigate another Arab). After being questioned by a security agent, the Muslim woman made a beeline for the Christian lady and verbally assaulted her. Refusing to address her in Arabic, the Muslim accused the Christian of “being a Jew” due to her support of Israel and said she should be ashamed. She then threatened that “we know who you are, and we will lay our hands on you.” The Christian lady later told us that such confrontations are not uncommon, and that there are Muslim groups that keep tabs on Arabs like her to harass and even harm any that dare show love and support for Israel. That caused me to think about the tremendous amount of courage demonstrated by Arabs like this amazing woman traveling with us. Sure, we are putting ourselves in a relative amount of danger by living in Israel and openly siding with the Jewish state. But the threat to us as foreign Christians does not even come close to that faced by Middle East Arabs who throw in their lot with Israel. This particular woman could very easily keep her mouth shut and remain at home, quietly loving Israel in her heart, while avoiding the very real and deadly threat posed to her and her family. But she is a true believer, a true servant of the Lord, and she knows that she has no choice but to serve the Lord openly and declare His truth regarding this nation… even at the cost of her own life. Pray for this woman, for her family and for the others like her who love the Lord and the “apple of His eye” more than their own lives.
