A Slimmer, More Focused Zionist.com

Jul 14th, 2007

Now that things are starting to settle down and I am getting into a routine at my new job, I have every intention of resuming my daily writing here at Zionist.com. Those familiar with Zionist.com will notice a few minor changes to the site. I have significantly slimmed down the site in terms of features. The truth is that Zionist.com does not support my family and I, and therefore must take a back seat in terms of attention and devoted work time to my paying job. As readership here increases and Zionist.com does begin to provide a degree of financial support, I will gradually increase the amount of attention the site receives, including adding new features and resources, until Zionist.com is the full-fledged, Bible-based, pro-Israel community site I always intended it to be. Until then, I want to focus on producing the core content - daily commentary on what is happening here on the ground - and not be distracted by trying to do too many other things that I simply don’t have time for. Anyway, enough of my rambling. The bottom line is that Zionist.com is more focused now, and will again be updated on a daily basis. So check back regularly!
