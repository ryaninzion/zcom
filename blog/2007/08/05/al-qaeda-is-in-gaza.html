Al Qaeda Is in Gaza

Aug 5th, 2007

Naturally, the Palestinian Authority and Gaza’s Hamas rulers want to discredit the notion that Osama bin Laden’s Al Qaeda terrorist network has set up shop in the territories under their control, but their claims simply are not compatible with the available evidence. Omedia did a recent analysis showing fairly definitively that the Gaza-based Army of Islam terror group is a local Al Qaeda branch, and that it is and has for years been operating in the volatile coastal strip with the approval of first the Fatah-dominated Palestinian Authority and now Hamas. > In an interview on the Ilaf website (July 17, 2007), Abu Ashour, the right-hand man of Army of Islam leader Mamtuz Doghmush, said the organization “embraces the principles in which Al Qaeda believes”… He noted Al Qaeda sends money to finance the activity of the Army of Islam, as well as conveying directives for action. Ashour believes “the decision to [BBC reporter] kidnap Alan Johnston was made by Al Qaeda ‘on the outside’ and the decision to release him also arrived from ‘outside’ in order to avoid bloodshed.” > > Abu Ashour also revealed the Army of Islam was established in the Gaza Strip 30 years ago. “At first the Afghans arrived in order to spread religious awareness,” he said, referring to Arab fighters who had fought in Afghanistan. “All the Afghans belonged to Al Qaeda and we called them by their noms de guerre.” That the Army of Islam operates in Gaza with the approval and even support of the local rulers became evident last month when Hamas and the Al Qaeda-linked group worked out a mutually beneficial deal culminating in Johnston’s release. While the Army of Islam claims to have been operating in Gaza for the past 30 years, its recent rise to prominence is evidence that any territory surrendered by Israel to the Palestinians will become a haven of global Islamic terrorism. Hamas is already too entrenched in every area of Palestinian society, be it in Gaza or Judea and Samaria. That much is clear following Hamas’ landslide municipal and parliamentary electoral victories over the past three years. Nor has American backing proved effective is stopping Hamas’ spread and eventual takeover of areas handed to the Palestinian Authority. If the so-called “West Bank” is given to the Palestinians, it will eventually be a Hamas stronghold, just like Gaza. And once that area is in Hamas hands, it will become a base of operations for Al Qaeda, a new headquarters from which Osama bin Laden’s ilk can plan their assault on Judeo-Christian civilization. Hamas and all the other Palestinian terror groups have successfully convinced the world that they are not part of the global Islamic jihad, and that their violence is directed solely against the “Israeli occupation.” That is why the presence of Al Qaeda is so detrimental to their current plans. What the Palestinians are trying to hide is that they ultimately share the ideology being put into action by Al Qaeda. > The Hamas movement is well aware of the growing power of the Al Qaeda offshoot and is troubled by the fact that quite a few in the Hamas military arm and even in its leadership have begun to embrace the global outlook of Al Qaeda. Hamas members especially are devout Muslims, and Islam teaches that they must subjugate the entire world, that all unbelievers must come under Islamic rule via military conquest. So it should be no surprise that elements within Hamas are starting to openly embrace Al Qaeda. It’s no surprise, but it should serve as a warning. These Hamas boys are just the beginning. Once the Palestinians have what they want from Israel, they will turn on their benefactors in the West.
