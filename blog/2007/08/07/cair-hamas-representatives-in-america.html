CAIR: Hamas' Representatives in America

Aug 7th, 2007

Think the Palestinian Authority and neighboring Arab dictatorships are the only places a group like Hamas can gain official representation and influence over the government? Think again, because apparently Hamas has already got its claws in the US administration and America’s top law enforcement agency. I typically focus on goings on here in Israel, so forgive my ignorance up until now of certain aspects of this post that may have long been common knowledge to many readers. In the wake of 9/11 and amidst the global war on Islamic terror, most are familiar with the Council on American-Islamic Relations (CAIR), a major US-based Muslim organization that claims to foster understanding of Islam, reconciliation, etc. But it turns out CAIR is a representative of the same violent Islamic ideology espoused by Hamas. Again, many will already be familiar with that fact, given that a number of CAIR staffers have been indicted on terrorism-related charges and the organization itself was recently named as an unindicted co-conspirator in the trial of the Texas-based Holy Land Foundation over its financial support of Hamas. It turns out CAIR may be even more than a mere admirer of Hamas - it may be an outright front for the Palestinian terror group. I was surprised to learn that CAIR was born out of the now-defunct Islamic Association for Palestine (IAP), a group that was started by a top Hamas leader and an Arab-American university professor that recently pleaded guilty to aiding the Palestinian Islamic Jihad. The same ideology that guided the IAP is now guiding CAIR. What makes this issue most disturbing is that instead of putting CAIR on a watch list or disbanding it all together, the US government has all but recognized it as the legitimate representative of all Muslims in America. Washington has gone so far as to grant CAIR a seat on the FBI’s advisory board, the result being that America’s top law enforcement agency cannot move on the community that by far spawns the most domestic and international terrorists without first consulting Hamas’ representatives in the US! While the US is focusing on defeating Islamic terrorism and its state sponsors abroad, Mohammed’s cohorts are rapidly positioning themselves to substantially alter America’s way of life from within.
