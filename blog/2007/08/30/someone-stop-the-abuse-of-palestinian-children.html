Someone Stop the Abuse of Palestinian Children!

Aug 30th, 2007

International human rights organizations are so focused on finding ways to demonize Israel that they are almost completely ignoring the daily illegal use of Palestinian children in terrorist activities. Three more Palestinian children were killed and a fourth was arrested this week while being cynically employed by Hamas. The only condemnation of these human rights violations came from the Israel Defense Forces (IDF), which was forced to act against the youngsters in order to protect Israeli lives. Late Wednesday night, Israeli forces operating in northern Gaza intercepted a 15-year-old Palestinian boy sent by Hamas on a suicide bomb mission against Israelis. He was carrying two explosive devices. On Thursday morning, Hamas sent three children, cousins between the ages of 10 and 12, to adjust several rocket launchers aimed at southern Israel. Israeli forces detected the movement around the launchers, anticipated a rocket barrage on southern Israel, and struck first. The army immediately issued a statement deploring the terrorists’ cowardly sacrifice of children to carry out tasks they themselves would not dare undertake for fear of physical harm. A military correspondent revealed on Army Radio that children sent to retrieve and adjust Hamas rocket launchers are usually paid the paltry sum of 10 shekels (about $2.50) for risking their lives. It is evidence of the sick state of the international community that these incidents have not resulted in massive and constant pressure on Hamas and the Palestinians in general to love their children enough to not employ them or allow them to be employed in terrorist activity. They can start by reforming the education system in the Palestinian-controlled territories to _not_ glorify death in the name of Allah. Of course, to do that they would need to censure Islam itself.
