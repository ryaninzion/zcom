What Islam Plans for Us All

Aug 6th, 2007

In a very disturbing report published this week we learn that a female Palestinian Christian professor from the Gaza Strip has been forced to convert to Islam by her Muslim university colleagues. WorldNetDaily’s Aaron Klein reports: > Sana al-Sayegh, head of the Science and Technology Department at Gaza City’s Palestine International University, disappeared June 24, failing to show up at work where she serves as the Gaza Strip’s only female doctorate in her field. > > … > > According to sources close to her family, about five days after she disappeared Sayegh caller her parents to say she was being held against her will in order to marry a Muslim man, who was also a professor at the university. What’s worse is that the university’s president, a known Islamist with ties to Gaza’s Hamas rulers, was one of the signatories to Sayegh’s conversion certificate, which was delivered to her family by armed Hamas militants who insisted the conversion was a matter of free will. Of course, this should not come as too much of a surprise, unless you are among the most blind of apologists for the Palestinians and Islam in general. Hamas is a devout Muslim group, and their Koran teaches them that the entire world must either convert to or be subjugated by Islam. Why not start with the infidel Christians in their midst? Sayegh returned to work this week, but has avoided contacting her family, probably under threat of death, and attempts by her family to contact her new “husband” have failed. Klein goes on to note that this incident took place just a little more than a month after a Hamas official said Gaza’s 2,000 Christians could continue to live there only if they submitted to strict Islamic law. Very few if any from that tiny community of Christians have dared to speak openly to the outside world since then. As usual, we must now ask, where are those champions of personal freedom and human rights that so readily jump on Israel over every alleged violation, no matter how minor? Why are the Christians of Gaza not worthy of their aid? With virtually no opposition, it is only a matter of time before Islam succeeds (temporarily) in achieving its goals of domination.
