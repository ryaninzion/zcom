The Jews Have Mind Control!

Aug 19th, 2007

That is the kind of headline I expect to see on all those anti-Semitic websites hiding behind the mask of being critical of Israel when they read the news that Israeli scientists have discovered how to erase long-term memory. It just fits so well with all those “the Jews are trying to secretly rule the world” conspiracies. Anyway, while those nut jobs are howling about how the Jews are coming to suck out their brains, they will have missed yet another astonishing medical breakthrough by God’s Chosen. In recent tests, researchers at Israel’s Weizmann Institute of Science managed to isolate long-term memories in the brains of rats and then used an enzyme inhibitor to block the protein that moves information related to those memories. The rats immediately forgot about a particular food the researchers had trained them to recognize as making them sick. It is just the first step on a long road to developing treatments for humans suffering from dementia, defective memory or a whole range of other memory-related ailments. But it is an impressive step. The disproportionate number of technological and medical advances that have come out of Israel is amazing. Despite Israel’s flaws and many mistakes, Paul says that God has blessed them for their fathers’ sakes (Romans 11:28), and we can certainly see the evidence of that.
