America Enabling Palestinian Terror

Aug 22nd, 2007

We all know that a large bulk of US taxpayers’ dollars sent to the Palestinian Authority ultimately go to finance acts of terrorism against Israelis. But the Palestinians say that even with the cash, in the absence of US military training, they could have never killed so many Israelis over the past seven years. A senior member of Palestinian Authority leader Mahmoud Abbas’ Presidential Guard had this to say in a recent interview with _The New York Sun_: > I do not think that the operations of the Palestinian resistance would have been so successful and would have killed more than one thousand Israelis since 2000…without these [American] trainings.” The official noted that the high Israeli body count had directly contributed to Israel’s decision to flee the Gaza Strip in 2005, marking a clear victory for American-enabled Palestinian terrorism. Not only does this show that far from combatting global Islamic terrorism, the US is actually facilitating it in some parts of the world, it also debunks the myth that the Palestinians see their seven-year-old terrorist uprising as a catastrophic disaster. How could they see it as such when they are now closer than ever to receiving a sovereign state on pretty much every inch of land they are currently demanding? No, the terrorist offensive has worked, and, as the Palestinian official quoted above noted, it owes its success to America. Oh, and in case some were confused, US training has aided Palestinian terrorism because the majority of Palestinian Authority security officers are also members of Palestinian terrorist organizations. They take their US-supplied weapons and training and turn around and kill Jews with them. But has Washington taken any of this to heart? Absolutely not. Just this month, the US State Department signed a deal to provide even more advanced training to Abbas’ forces out of fear of a Hamas takeover in Judea and Samaria (the so-called “West Bank”). Of course, Hamas already proved that its Iranian training trumps the American training provided to Abbas’ forces when it conquered the Gaza Strip last June despite being vastly outnumbered. So one of two things will now happen in Judea and Samaria: 1. Hamas will defeat Abbas’ forces despite their new training and weapons and will become a mini-Iran in Israel’s own backyard; or 2. Abbas’ forces will manage to retain control in Judea and Samaria, and will be equipped to kill another thousand Jews the moment their leadership gives the green light. Either way, bad news for Israel, and shame on America.
