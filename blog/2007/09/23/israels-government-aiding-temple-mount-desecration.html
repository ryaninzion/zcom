Israel's Government Aiding Temple Mount Desecration

Sep 23rd, 2007

Amazingly, after Jews around the world dreamt and prayed for 2,000 years about returning to Zion and even rebuilding their temple to the Almighty, an Israeli government is today actively shielding the Muslim powers that are trying to erase any Jewish connection to Jerusalem’s Temple Mount. This past Friday, Israel’s High Court heard the response of Prime Minister Ehud Olmert’s government to an earlier petition by leading archeologists to have the authorities halt the destructive excavations and renovations being carried out without supervision by the Muslim Trust (Waqf) that oversees the Temple Mount. To the shock and dismay of the archeologists, the judge ordered them out of the courtroom so that he could be presented with “secret evidence” by Olmert’s government “proving” that the Muslims had caused no damage to any Jewish antiquities. Why would these archeologists be ordered out of the courtroom unless the government either didn’t really have any evidence or the evidence they did have didn’t really prove anything? What seems most likely to me is that the petitioners were ordered out so that the Olmert government could explain to the judge without any interruptions or objections why Israel needs to just keep its mouth shut and allow its enemies to destroy all remains of a historical Jewish connection to the Temple Mount so as not to disrupt “peace” efforts. Earlier this month, Israeli television showed footage of the Muslim excavations atop the Temple Mount. In the footage, a deep and long gash can be seen in the floor of the compound, revealing at least one ornate ancient wall that experts say is likely the first piece of the Second Temple to be revealed in centuries. The wall was smashed during the dig. It is no secret that the Muslims want to deny any Jewish connection to this most holy place. They say as much all the time. What is shocking is that a Jewish Israeli government is going out of its way to shield the Muslims in this despicable act of rewriting history. A couple weeks ago, the Temple Institute in Jerusalem called on all Bible-believing Christians to rise up and aid their Jewish brothers in defending the Temple Mount against the plans of Allah. I think it is time we answer that call, since the needed help is clearly not going to come from Israel’s own authorities.
