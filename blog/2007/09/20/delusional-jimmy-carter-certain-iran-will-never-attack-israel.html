Delusional Jimmy Carter Certain Iran Will Never Attack Israel

Sep 20th, 2007

Jimmy Carter this week continued his rise as the new king of the delusional leftists who insist Israel and the US have no real enemies besides those they themselves provoke to hostile action. In an address at Emory University, Carter stated adamantly that it was “almost inconceivable” that Iran would actually attack Israel, and insisted that the Islamic Republic does not pose any real threat to either the Jewish state or the US. Said Carter: > Iran is quite distant from Israel. Uh, ever heard of ballistic missiles? > I think it would be almost inconceivable that Iran would commit suicide by launching one or two missiles of any kind against the nation of Israel. Ok, so he has heard of ballistic missiles, but apparently missed the memo that Iran has 600 of them pointed at Israel, not just two. Unbelievably, this is the same Jimmy Carter who was president during the Islamic Revolution and the seizing of the US Embassy in Tehran. And he somehow now finds it impossible to believe that the same kind of Islamic madmen who perpetrated those crimes would ever launch an attack on the Islamic world’s most hated enemy. This is exactly the kind of delusional wishful thinking that resulted in British-led Western Europe keeping its eyes tightly closed until Hitler’s Germany had actually launched its conquest of the continent.
