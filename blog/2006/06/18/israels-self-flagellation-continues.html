Israel's Self-flagellation Continues

Jun 18th, 2006

Despite evidence that Israel could not possibly have been responsible for the deaths of seven “Palestinian” family members on a Gaza beach last week, the Jewish state’s self-proclaimed leftist “elites” continue to condemn both themselves and their nation. The lastest to join in this act of self-flagellation is popular TV talk show host Yair Lapid, who wrote in an editorial published by _Yediot Ahronot_ that he is “ashamed. Deeply, painfully humiliated, ashamed of us and what we have done, where we have ended up. …because we have become child killers.” The only “proof” that Israel was responsible for those deaths are the unsubstantiated claims of the “Palestinians” themselves. And their word has not often proved to be of any value. You know, Yair, in addition to being “child killers,” the Arabs also insist you Jews are the sons of monkeys and pigs. Do their assertions also make that true?
