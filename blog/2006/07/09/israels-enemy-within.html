Israel's Enemy Within

Jul 9th, 2006

A large portion of Israel’s Arab population has long applauded, supported and even participated in “Palestinian” terrorism against their Jewish countrymen. With Israel’s Arab parliamentarians becoming more and more bold in their treasonous disdain for Jewish life and Israel’s continued survival, that phenomenon can only grow. Balad Party MK Wasil Taha was recently caught urging the “Palestinians” on an online Islamic chat room to focus their attacks on the IDF, and even to attempt the abduction of Jewish soldiers. > “We told them more than once that the Palestinians…have two options: One is to explode and kill civilians - and these are much easier operations. The second option is the military option, in the framework of which a military unit from the resistance [terrorist groups] is established in order to break into a military camp, clash with the soldiers and battle them and take them and the conquest into captivity.” Now, it is difficult to determine if Taha’s advice led directly to IDF Corporal Gilad Shalit’s abduction, but the timing seems too close to be a coincidence. During an interview on Israel Radio Sunday, Taha’s refusal to retract his original remarks indicated he would have no problem with his words acting as the inspiration behind Shalit’s ongoing terrifying ordeal. Taha was followed by former MK Abdulmalik Dehamshe of the United Arab List who told the Israel-Arab newspaper _Kul al-Arab_ that “Palestinian” rocket attacks, terrorist abductions and “suicide” bombings are “legitimate and respected.” > “The Palestinians have no other weapons but these rockets and the suicide bombings … if it wasn’t for the suicide bombings â€“ the Palestinian issue would be dead by now.” How far do you let “free speech” go before you start hanging people for treason?
