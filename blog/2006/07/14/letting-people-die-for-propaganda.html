Letting People Die for Propaganda

Jul 14th, 2006

It is a practice most in the West cannot comprehend. That a government would openly take action that causes the death and suffering of is own people is truly vile. But it is also entirely common in the Muslim Middle East, especially when propaganda points are on the table. Over the past two weeks, thousands of Palestinian Arabs have been stranded in Egyptian Sinai, unable to reenter the Gaza Strip. They have been living without shelter and an organized supply of food and water. Eight have died as a result of the conditions. The Palestinian Authority and its co-conspirators in the international media would have us believe Israel is holding the gates shut, purposely creating this humanitarian situation. But none other than a small group of “Palestinian” terrorists were kind enough to show us the truth Thursday, when they attacked the PA Presidential Guard soldiers who are keeping the border closed. Now, it is true that they are keeping it closed because Israel has demanded such action in order to prevent its abducted soldier from being taken out of Gaza. But Israel has also tried to alleviate the humanitarian situation by opening another border crossing - one which can be better monitored by Israel. Naturally, the PA rejected the offer, preferring to see its own people die than let the Jews do something to help them.
