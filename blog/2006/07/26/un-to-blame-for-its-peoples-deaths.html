UN to Blame for Its People's Deaths

Jul 26th, 2006

Kofi Annan and his buddies are trying really hard to keep news of Israel’s accidental killing of four UN peacekeepers in southern Lebanon on the front pages. But the truth is, the UN itself is to blame for the deaths of those four international troops. Israeli bombs may have (unintentionally) taken their lives, but if UNIFIL (the UN Interim Force In Lebanon) had done the job it was given 28 years ago, Israel wouldn’t have to be dropping bombs at all today. UNIFIL was given a mandate in 1978 to keep hostile forces away from northern Israel and help the Lebanese government extend its authority over southern Lebanon. It failed miserably (or didn’t even try), leaving Israel with no choice but to invade in 1982 and push Yasser Arafat’s PLO out of range of the Jewish state. In May 2000, Israel gave UNIFIL another chance by vacating southern Lebanon. But again, the “peacekeeping” force refused to do anything about Hizb’allah’s control of southern Lebanon and aggression against Israel. In fact, not only did UN troops sit by and watch as Hizb’allah abducted three IDF soldiers in October 2000, it was later discovered that the terrorists actually used UNIFIL vehicles to enable them to get close enough to the Israelis undetected. But Israel, in its deep desire to see the international community live up to its word, stayed out of Lebanon and continued to entrust the safety of its northern residents to an impotent foreign force. But the massive assault earlier this month was the straw that finally broke the camel’s back. Why did UNIFIL not prevent Hizb’allah from launching that provocation? It’s almost as if the world wants Israel to get sucked into these orgies of violence just so it has another chance to condemn the Jewish state. UNIFIL could have avoided yesterday’s casualties by having done its job in the first place. And after deciding not to do its job, UNIFIL should have promptly removed its troops from the area when it became clear Israel would have to the job for it. Helping to facilitate the creation of a war zone, then placing your own people right in the middle of it, and then blaming the nation suffering aggression out of that war zone for accidentally killing your people is utter foolishness.
